My Approach

As a front-end developer, I believe in building clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My approach includes the following steps:

  • Understand the Client's Needs: I always start by listening carefully to the client's goals and expectations.
  • Plan and Design: I create wireframes and prototypes to visualize the design before development starts.
  • Development & Testing: I build websites with clean, efficient code, ensuring they are responsive and optimized for all devices.
  • Feedback & Iteration: I work closely with clients to gather feedback and make any necessary improvements.
